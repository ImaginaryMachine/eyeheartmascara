Face Steam

An amazing way to open your pores is too have your own little sauna  for your face! 
Ingredients*
Boiling Water
Herbs (rosemary, lemon grass)

Boil some water either in a pot or in a tea heater or anything else that heats up water :). Carefully pour it into a bowl  and add your herbs. Lower your face near the bowl and put a towel over your head. Wait for about 15-20 min and pat face dry. Your pores are now open and you can apply your moisturizer or serum and it will sink into your pores.
