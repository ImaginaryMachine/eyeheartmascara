Face Masks

Honey Face Mask-
Ingredients*
Oats
Honey
Milk
Vanilla Extract (for that amazing smell)

Put your oats in a food processor or grinding machine and grind your oats till they're about half their original size.
Pour grounded oats into a bowl and add milk little by little till it reaches the consistency where it has a bit of milk in it but not to watery either. Add a desired amount of honey (not to much or it will make your face super sticks ;). Next add just the tiniest amount of vanilla to give it that amazing smell. It should a consistency that you think you would be able to put on your face. Leave it on for about 15-20 min. Careful not too move around much because it might start falling off your face! It should feel pretty dry, then wash it off. Your face should feel soft and re hydrated! Apply your moisturizer and your good to go!
